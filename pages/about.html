About

This is a website dedicated to elect_club, a club for elected electronic music producers/musicians alike, located primarily in Victoria, Australia. You may see here the ideas that the club is built upon, the projects we create/will create, how to join, etc.

The club is specifically designed and cater to foster creativity given the following ideas.

Open-Source Music Concept: I propose applying open-source software principles (like FOSS) directly to music creation and the music industry. This approach, largely unexplored in music, offers immense potential to counter the industry's profit-driven limitations, commodification of average art, and barriers to creativity. It aims to make music creation more accessible and collaborative, envisioning a platform like "GitHub for music."

Key Personal Production Strategies:

  1. Low-Fidelity Focus: Restrict early project listening to low-fidelity speakers (e.g., laptop speakers, NS10s) to isolate the mid-range. This forces the mix to mature before testing on high-fidelity systems, ensuring it translates well universally.
  2. Minimum Song Commitment: Set a mandatory minimum time to work on a single song before switching projects. This combats my tendency to abandon tracks prematurely and fosters completion.

Call to Action: I aim to host in-person meetups for passionate producers seeking community beyond commercial pressures. Our dreams are ours to follow. I have developed methods to create uniquely creative music, moving past the constraints I previously accepted.
